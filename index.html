End-to-End HR Solutions

QT Consultancy (OPC) Pvt Ltd — formerly known as Qalithar Consultancy (OPC) Pvt Ltd — provides end-to-end recruitment, payroll processing, onboarding, employee benefits administration and manpower migration services across India.

  • Expertise in both blue-collar and white-collar talent acquisition.
  • Commitment to statutory compliance (PF, ESIC, Taxes).
  • Tailored strategies to match your unique company culture.
  • A large national network for faster, more efficient hiring.
